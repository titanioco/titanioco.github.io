## Access summary

An ongoing learning group focused on understanding Bitcoin and cryptocurrency fundamentals.
We keep it practical: how Bitcoin works, basic security, wallets, transactions, and how to evaluate crypto projects critically.

## What we do

- Explain key concepts with simple mental models (UTXO vs account model, blocks, fees, confirmations)
- Practice safe habits (seed phrases, cold vs hot wallets, common scams)
- Read and discuss real-world news with a technical/financial lens
- Build small exercises (paper wallet concepts, transaction tracking, basic on-chain explorers)

## Calls to action

1) Get the fundamentals (free)
- Bitcoin whitepaper (short): https://bitcoin.org/bitcoin.pdf
- Bitcoin.org basics: https://bitcoin.org/en/getting-started

2) Learn security basics (free)
- Common scams and best practices (overview): https://bitcoin.org/en/protect-your-wallet

3) Track one real transaction (practice)
- Pick a transaction from your own wallet history (or a public example) and follow it in an explorer
- Note: fee rate, confirmation count, and inputs/outputs

## Format (simple and sustainable)

- Ongoing sessions
- Weekly or bi-weekly (small group)
- Bring: one question, one news link, and one concept you want clarified
