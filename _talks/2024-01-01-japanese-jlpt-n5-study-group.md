## Access summary

An ongoing (since 2024) study group to learn Japanese fundamentals for JLPT N5.
We focus on consistency: kana fluency, core grammar, high-frequency vocabulary, and simple listening/reading using free resources.

## What we do

- Kana practice (hiragana/katakana) + reading speed
- Core N5 grammar patterns in short, reusable sentences
- Vocabulary practice (frequency-first) + mini dialogues
- Listening from beginner-friendly material + shadowing

## Calls to action

1) Get to kana fluency (daily, 10 minutes)
- Learn Hiragana: https://www.tofugu.com/japanese/learn-hiragana/
- Learn Katakana: https://www.tofugu.com/japanese/learn-katakana/

2) Follow one free grammar track consistently
- Tae Kim’s Guide to Japanese (free): https://guidetojapanese.org/learn/

3) Add simple reading/listening that’s sustainable
- NHK News Web Easy: https://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/easy/

## Dictionary (free)

- Jisho (EN–JA dictionary): https://jisho.org/

## Format (simple and sustainable)

- Ongoing (started in 2024)
- Weekly or bi-weekly sessions (small group)
- Bring: 10 new words, 3 grammar points, and 5 example sentences

If you want, tell me whether you’re aiming for JLPT N5 specifically (date) or “general beginner Japanese”, and I’ll tailor the weekly plan.
